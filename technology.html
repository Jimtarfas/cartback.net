Security & Compliance

Enterprise Security Standards

Your data is protected by industry-leading security practices and compliance certifications.

Data Encryption

All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Customer PII is tokenized and stored in isolated, encrypted databases with strict access controls.

SOC 2 Type II

Independently audited security controls covering data protection, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, and privacy. Annual third-party audits by a Big Four firm.

GDPR & CCPA Compliant

Full compliance with international data protection regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and LGPD. Automated data subject access requests and right-to-erasure handling.

Meta Platform Compliance

Full adherence to Meta's Platform Terms, Data Use Policy, and Business Tool Terms. Regular compliance reviews and data processing agreements in place with Meta.

Cloud Infrastructure

Hosted on AWS with multi-region redundancy, auto-scaling, and disaster recovery. 99.99% uptime SLA with 24/7 infrastructure monitoring and incident response.

Access Controls

Role-based access control (RBAC) with multi-factor authentication. All system access is logged, monitored, and auditable. Regular penetration testing by certified ethical hackers.
