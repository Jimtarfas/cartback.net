Specialists in the Meta Ecosystem

Security, Privacy & Compliance

Building Software the Right Way

Every project we deliver follows secure development practices and respects the privacy & data-use policies of the platforms we build on.

Secure by Design

Encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3), secrets management (Vault/AWS SM), role-based access control, and OWASP Top 10 mitigations on every project.

Meta Platform Policy Compliance

Strict adherence to Meta Platform Terms, Data Use Policy, Developer Policies, App Review guidelines, WhatsApp Business Messaging Policy, and advertising standards.

GDPR & CCPA Aware

Privacy-by-design across architecture decisions. We implement data subject access workflows, right-to-erasure, consent management, and data minimization patterns.

Code Review & CI/CD

Every commit is peer-reviewed. Automated linting, type checks, unit/integration tests, dependency scanning, and security checks run on every pull request.

Reliable Cloud Infrastructure

Multi-AZ deployments, auto-scaling, infrastructure-as-code (Terraform), disaster recovery planning, and 24/7 monitoring with on-call escalation paths.

Team Confidentiality

All engineers and contractors sign NDAs and IP assignment agreements. Project data is segregated, access-controlled, and securely destroyed at project close.
