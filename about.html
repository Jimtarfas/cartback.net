About CartBack

Building the Future of E-Commerce Recovery

We're a team of marketing technologists, data scientists, and e-commerce experts on a mission to help online businesses maximize every customer interaction.

Our Story

From Problem to Platform

CartBack was founded with a simple observation: e-commerce businesses lose billions of dollars annually to abandoned shopping carts, yet most lack the technology and expertise to recover that revenue effectively.

Our founders — with decades of combined experience in e-commerce, advertising technology, and the Meta platform ecosystem — built CartBack to bridge that gap. Starting as a cart recovery tool, we've grown into a comprehensive marketing technology platform trusted by over 500 businesses worldwide.

Today, as an official Meta Technology Provider, we integrate deeply with Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, and Messenger to help brands reach customers where they are — and bring them back to complete their purchases.

Founded in 2019

Building e-commerce recovery technology for over 6 years.
