About Cart Back

A Software Agency Built by Engineers

We're a multidisciplinary team of software engineers, product designers, and platform specialists building software that businesses depend on every day.

Our Story

From a Small Team to a Trusted Partner

Cart Back was founded by software engineers who believed that high-quality custom software development should be accessible to businesses of every size — not just enterprises with eight-figure budgets.

What began as a small consulting practice has grown into a multidisciplinary software development agency with engineers, designers, DevOps specialists, and platform experts working together on web applications, mobile apps, and complex platform integrations.

Today, Cart Back is a registered U.S. business headquartered in Wasilla, Alaska, serving clients across North America, Europe, and beyond. We are particularly recognized for our deep, hands-on expertise across the Meta platform ecosystem — building WhatsApp Business Platform integrations, Messenger experiences, Facebook Marketing API tools, and custom Meta apps for clients in retail, healthcare, finance, education, and more.

Compliance & Standards

Built on a Foundation of Trust

We hold ourselves to high standards of compliance, security, and platform integrity.

Meta Platform Compliance

All software we build that integrates with Meta platforms follows Meta's Platform Terms, Data Use Policy, App Review Guidelines, and advertising standards.

Registered U.S. Business

Cart Back is a legally registered business in the United States, with a verifiable address, phone, and tax registration — ready for B2B and platform partnerships.

Security-First Engineering

Encryption at rest and in transit, role-based access control, dependency scanning, and secure code review for every project we deliver.

Every line of production code is peer-reviewed. Automated CI/CD, linting, type checks, and tests are baseline expectations on every project.
