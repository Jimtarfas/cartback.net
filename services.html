More Services

Cloud, Design, and Strategy

Beyond development, we provide the cloud infrastructure, design polish, and strategic consulting that make great software successful.

Cloud & DevOps

We architect and operate cloud infrastructure on AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure — including CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure-as-code (Terraform), Kubernetes, observability, and 24/7 monitoring. Migrations, cost optimization, and security hardening included.

UX & UI Design

How We Engage

Engagement Models That Fit Your Project

Whether you need a one-time project, a dedicated team, or ongoing support — we offer flexible engagement models.

Fixed-Scope Projects

Best for well-defined initiatives with clear requirements. We agree on scope, timeline, and budget upfront — and deliver on it. Perfect for MVPs, platform integrations, and discrete feature builds.

Dedicated Teams

Embed a full Cart Back team — engineers, designers, PM — as an extension of your organization. Best for long-term product development where you need consistent capacity and deep context.

Retainer & Support

Ongoing engineering capacity for maintenance, enhancements, bug fixes, and platform updates. Ideal once your software is in production and you need a reliable engineering partner.
